# 2026 Award Ceremony[¶](#award-ceremony "Permalink to this heading")

The 2026 Award Ceremony will be held on April 24 at the [MITRE Bedford campus](https://www.mitre.org/sites/default/files/2024-01/MITRE-Campus-Bedford_01-24.pdf).
We are anticipating an early afternoon start time, but the exact time is TBA.

The Award Ceremony is an opportunity to share all the great work teams have done
over the course of the comnpetition and to reveal the final standings. Select
teams will be invited to present their work to the MITRE organizers,
sponsors, and their fellow competitors.

Note

More information about and registration for this event will be released closer to the event.
