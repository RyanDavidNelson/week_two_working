# Schedule[¶](#schedule "Permalink to this heading")

## Phases[¶](#phases "Permalink to this heading")

* [Design Phase](../../about/index.html#design-phase) Teams design and build their own secure design
* [Handoff](../../about/index.html#handoff): Teams turn in their design to the eCTF organizers for testing
* [Attack Phase](../../about/index.html#attack-phase): Teams that have passed Handoff can earn points by
  attacking the designs of other teams in the Attack Phase

![../../_images/eCTF%20Phases.png](images/schedule__index__eCTF_20Phases.png)

## Workshop Schedule[¶](#workshop-schedule "Permalink to this heading")

The eCTF organizers will hold routine workshops to provide competitors with information
about different parts of the competition. Monitor the announcements channel on
[Zulip](../../about/zulip.html) for details. Unless otherwise noted, all workshops will be held
from 1200 to 1300 Eastern Time.

* January 20: Boot Reference Design

* January 29: Docker Introduction

* February 10: Testing Service
