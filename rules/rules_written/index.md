# 2026 eCTF[¶](#ectf "Permalink to this heading")

Welcome to the 2026 eCTF!

This year, teams will create a solution for securely creating, storing,
and transferring sensitive integrated circuit fabrication data between
digital parties with varying levels of trust and permissions. The system
must not allow unauthorized creation, disclosure, or modification of the
IC design data.
